📩What happens after you submit
1
Automatic email notification

An email summary (including any attached documents) is sent immediately to you, the relevant Regional Feasibility Lead(s) for each selected region, and the Feasibility Admin. No need to resubmit — simply reply to that email to add further information or context.

2
Arrange a kick-off meeting

Following submission, it is recommended that the PM arrange a meeting with the relevant Region Head(s) and Regional Feasibility Leads to align on requirements before outreach commences.
